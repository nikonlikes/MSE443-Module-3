CONCLUSIONS
- Scheduling heuristic has the lowest mean sum_completion_times for all sizes.
- It wins on 100% of small and medium instances, and 70% of large instances.
- Greedy wins on 9/30 large instances; local_search never has the minimum.
- Variability (std) is much higher for greedy and local_search, especially for large.
